Mechanical Engineering Professional

I recently finished my master in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Purdue University on August, where I worked with Prof. Goldenstein on developing a single-ended laser-based sensor. My thesis title is Development of a Single-Ended Laser-Absorption-Spectroscopy Sensor for High-Temperature Gases. Besides, I am also interested in engine technology, electric vehicle and sensor technology for autonomous driving including Radar, camera, and LiDAR. During fall semester in 2017, I took Co-op as a Thermal and Fluid Science Engineer in Cummins, Seymour, where I performed 1D & 3D CFD analysis for high-horse power diesel engines and provided related analsyis support for engine design.
